Types of Manga

Are you having a hard time looking for comics that suit your taste? Try reading a manga, it is a style of Japanese comic book, is very popular among adults and children. It has 5 different types which are Shonen, Shoujo, Seinen, Josei, Kodomomuke. Shonen is made for teenage boys and are mostly action filled, Shoujo is for teenage girls and is mostly romance, Seinen is made for adult males and tends to be more violent, Josei is also a romance manga but is more realistic than shoujo manga, and lastly Kodomomuke is a manga made for young children and is mostly educational.
